## How to use

### Blocking a channel
1. Click the extension icon in your toolbar
2. Type the channel name exactly as it appears on YouTube (e.g. `NewsNation`)
3. Click **Add** or press Enter
4. The channel's videos will disappear from your feed immediately â€” no page reload needed
